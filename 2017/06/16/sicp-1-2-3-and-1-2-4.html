Section 1.2.3 - Orders of Growth

Exercise 1.14

The tree is big :)

The recursive process reaches maximum depth when the denomination is a penny, for example (cc 11 1) will be 111111. This means that the growth of space will be Θ(n)\Theta(n)Θ(n), where nnn is the amount we want to change.

For each denomination sub-tree, the maximum steps we will need to calculate is where the only remaining denomination is the penny, and the steps required will be exactly nnn. Since we have 555 denominations, the time complexity will be O(n5)O(n^5)O(n5).

b.
